## Subscribe to Plus

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit stenox.app">
    Go to [stenox.app/pricing](https://stenox.app/pricing) and select Plus
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Checkout">
    Payments are processed securely by [Polar](https://polar.sh)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive License Key">
    Your license key will be emailed immediately
  </Step>

  <Step title="Activate in Stenox">
    Open Stenox → Settings → **License** and sign in with your purchase email
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The email you use during checkout is used for license delivery, billing, and portal access.
</Note>

<Card title="Get Plus" icon="arrow-up-right-from-square" href="https://stenox.app/pricing">
  Subscribe to Plus at stenox.app
</Card>

## Manage Subscription

In Stenox Settings → **License**, you can:

- View subscription status
- Update payment method
- Cancel subscription

<Note>
  Canceling reverts you to the free tier (5 minutes/day). Your settings, profiles, and vocabulary are preserved.
</Note>
