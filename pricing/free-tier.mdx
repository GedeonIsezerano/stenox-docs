## Provider Options

The free tier gives you access to all provider types within your 5-minute daily limit:

### Stenox Cloud

Your $5 credit works with Stenox Cloud transcription and AI enhancement — no API keys needed.

### BYOK Providers

Use your own API keys from providers like:
- **Groq Whisper** — Fast transcription
- **Google Gemini** — AI enhancement
- **DeepGram** — High-accuracy transcription

### Local Providers

Process everything on-device for complete privacy:
- **WhisperKit** — Local transcription
- **MLX** — Local AI enhancement
- No internet required

[Set up local providers →](/providers/local-privacy)

## Free Tier FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens when I hit the limit?" icon="clock">
    Stenox will notify you that you've reached your daily limit. You can:
    - Wait until midnight for the reset
    - Upgrade to Lifetime for unlimited use
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does file transcription count against the limit?" icon="file-audio">
    Yes, file transcription time counts against your daily 5 minutes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use multiple providers?" icon="layer-group">
    Yes! Set up different providers for different profiles. All providers work within your daily limit.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I want complete privacy?" icon="lock">
    Local providers (WhisperKit + MLX) process everything on your Mac with no data sent anywhere. They work within your 5-minute daily limit on the free tier.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
