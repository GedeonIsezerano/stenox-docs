## Getting Your Free API Key

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit AssemblyAI">
    Go to [assemblyai.com](https://www.assemblyai.com) and click **Get Started Free** or **Sign Up**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up with:
    - Email address and password
    - Or use Google/GitHub sign-in

    **No credit card required!**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Check your email for a verification link from AssemblyAI and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access the Dashboard">
    Once logged in, you'll be redirected to the AssemblyAI Dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find your API key">
    Your API key is displayed prominently on the Dashboard:
    1. Look for the **API Key** section
    2. Click **Copy** to copy your key to clipboard
    3. Or click **Show** to reveal the full key
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add key to Stenox">
    1. Open Stenox Settings
    2. Go to **Transcription** tab
    3. Select **AssemblyAI** as provider
    4. Paste your API key
    5. Choose your model (Universal-1)
    6. Click **Save**
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Keep your API key private!** Don't share it or commit it to public repositories.
</Note>

## Available Models

### Universal-1 (Recommended)

AssemblyAI's flagship model optimized for accuracy across diverse scenarios:

**Universal-1 English:**
- Trained on diverse English accents (US, UK, Australian, Indian, African, etc.)
- Best for: English dictation with any accent

**Universal-1 Multilingual:**
- Supports English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese
- Auto-language detection
- Best for: Non-English languages or code-switching

<Tip>
  **Recommendation:** Use **Universal-1 English** for English dictation, or **Universal-1 Multilingual** if you speak multiple languages.
</Tip>

## Language Support

AssemblyAI supports multiple languages with auto-detection:

**Supported Languages:**
- **English** - All accents and dialects (US, UK, Australian, Indian, etc.)
- **Spanish** - Spain and Latin American variants
- **French** - France and Canadian variants
- **German**
- **Italian**
- **Portuguese** - Portugal and Brazilian variants
- **Auto-detect** - Automatically identify language being spoken

Configure language in Stenox profile settings.

<Steps>

## Features

### Accent Optimization

AssemblyAI's Universal-1 model excels with diverse accents:

- Non-native English speakers
- Regional accents (Southern US, Boston, Scottish, etc.)
- International English (Indian, Nigerian, Singaporean, etc.)
- Mixed accent environments

<Tip>
  If you have a strong accent or speak with non-native pronunciation, AssemblyAI often provides better accuracy than other providers.
</Tip>

### Streaming Transcription

AssemblyAI uses WebSocket streaming for real-time results:

- Audio streams to AssemblyAI as you speak
- Processing begins immediately
- Results return in < 1-2 seconds after you stop speaking

### Automatic Features

**Punctuation:**
- Automatic periods, commas, question marks
- Natural capitalization
- Sentence structure optimization

**Formatting:**
- Numbers formatted appropriately (e.g., "twenty-five" ‚Üí "25")
- Dates and times formatted correctly
- Currency and units handled intelligently

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="API key not working" icon="key">
    - Verify you copied the complete key from AssemblyAI Dashboard
    - Check that your account is verified (email confirmation)
    - Try generating a new API key in the Dashboard
    - Ensure no extra spaces or characters were added
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Transcription fails or errors" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    - Check your internet connection
    - Verify you have remaining credits in Dashboard
    - Ensure audio is being captured (check Stenox recording overlay)
    - Try a shorter test recording first
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Poor accuracy for your accent" icon="microphone-slash">
    - AssemblyAI is generally excellent with accents, but try:
      - Speaking slightly more clearly
      - Reducing background noise
      - Using a better microphone
      - Adding custom vocabulary in Stenox
    - Compare with DeepGram or WhisperKit for your specific accent
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Wrong language detected (auto-detect)" icon="globe">
    - Instead of auto-detect, manually select your language in Stenox profile settings
    - Speak longer phrases (auto-detect needs more context)
    - Ensure you're using Universal-1 Multilingual model
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
